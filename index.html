What is FIRST?

FIRST (For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology) is a global robotics organization founded by Dean Kamen and Woodie Flowers in 1989. FIRST's mission is to inspire young people to be science and technology leaders by engaging them in exciting mentor-based programs that build science, engineering, and technology skills.


FIRST programs help students discover and develop their passion for STEM while learning valuable life skills including teamwork, problem-solving, communication, and gracious professionalism. With programs for students from kindergarten through high school, FIRST offers something for everyone!
